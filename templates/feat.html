Mango Genome Database Pakistan

Mangifera Indica L.

A genomic resource of Mango database of Mangifera Indica L.

According to Food and Agriculture Organization (FAO) of United Nations, after banana, Mango is the dominant tropical fruit produced worldwide.

Anacardiaceae Family

Mango belongs to the family Anacardiaceae and found in many tropical and sub-tropical countries including China, Mexico, Indonesia, Israel, Malasia, Egypt and Indian subcontinents (India, Pakistan and Bangladesh).

Mango Genome Database

A Bioinformatics resource developed to provide Mango Genomic Information to the scientific community and general public.

About

This project is aimed to provide Bioinformatics resources related to Mango Genomic sequence information. This project is developed at the department of Bioinformatics, Sir syed university of Engineering & Technology, karachi Pakistan.

Tools

This website provides Bioinformatics tools for retrieval and analysis of Genomic sequences of different Mango cultivars (varieties).
